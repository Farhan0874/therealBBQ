The Real BBQ
Website Has To Look Good

The Real BBQ started from house backyard and soon it became popular among sub- continent community so serving masses from backyard was difficult to handle so a midterm solution was taken and initially The Real BBQ partnered with a local Mediterranean restaurant and served the community. Now The Real BBQ is honored to announce the opening of their own restaurant. The Real BBQ is known for its delicious, juicy & tender barbecue marinated in homemade spices and served straight from the charcoal grill.
